    • Welcome to MUKTI!

    Mukti is an vibrant FOSS community based in Bengaluru, India. We stand for the FOSS (Free and Open Source) Ideology, and promote cybersecurity, network security and software development within the FOSS movement. We are the largest Open-Source community in Bengaluru, recently touching a staggering 600 members. We remain committed to making, showcasing and promoting open-source contributions from members of our community.

    Get on-board!

    If you're passionate about open source, cybersecurity, and technology, we welcome you to join MUKTI. Together, we can make a difference in the world of technology.
